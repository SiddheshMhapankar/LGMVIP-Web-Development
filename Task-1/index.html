Photography is the art of creating durable images by recording light, by means of an image sensor, or by means of a photographic film.

HSL Sliders

HSL and HSV are alternative representations of the RGB color model, designed in the 1970s by computer graphics researchers to more closely align with the way human vision perceives color-making attributes.

Unsharp masking

Unsharp masking (USM) is an image sharpening technique, often available in digital image processing software. Its name derives from the fact that the technique uses a blurred, negative image to create a mask of the original image.

Image warping

Image warping is the process of digitally manipulating an image such that any shapes portrayed in the image have been significantly distorted. Warping may be used for correcting image distortion as well as for creative purposes.
